Blocked Calendar

"Blocked Calendar" offers a poignant and gripping account of the unprecedented lockdown imposed in India from students' perspective. In this compelling narrative, readers are taken on a journey through the eyes of young minds grappling with the sudden disruption of their lives. The book delves into the emotional, academic, and social challenges faced by students as they adapt to a new reality shaped by isolation, online learning, and uncertainty.
